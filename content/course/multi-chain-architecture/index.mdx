Are you ready to dive deeper into the fascinating world of Dijets? This course will equip you with a comprehensive understanding of the Dijets's Multi Blockchain architecture, along with the practical skills required to create and manage custom blockchains.

Ideal for people familiar with the basics of Dijets, this course will unlock the potential of creating custom blockchains. We will explore the various components and key concepts of the Custom Blockchain architecture, empowering you to fully leverage its benefits.

Throughout this course, you will gain hands-on experience by utilizing the Dijets Command Line Interface (Dijets CLI) to create your own custom blockchains and run them locally. You will explore different variations and configurations to learn the versatility and advantages of custom blockchains. Additionally, you will interact with the local instances using the Core Wallet, further enhancing your practical skills.

Let's get started exploring the power of Dijets Custom Blockchains.

## Prerequisites

This course is for people who understand the Dijets basics. If you are not familiar with Dijets Consensus, Virtual Machines, blockchains in Dijets, or Dijets Custom Blockchains, we strongly recommend taking our [Dijets Fundamentals](/course/dijets-fundamentals) course first.

## Learning Outcomes

In this course you will learn:

- The benefits of Dijets Custom Blockchains
- How to use Dijets CLI to create and run custom blockchains locally
- How to interact with Dijets ECCs using Core Wallet
